Photography

Select images that have a realistic, journalistic style. They should appear natural and spontaneous rather than posed or staged — i.e., not obviously “stock.” Images should be modern and dynamic, and should most often include a human element — either a person or the “suggestion” of human presence, such as a hand, a foot, part of a face, etc.
