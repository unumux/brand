Iconography

Selecting icons

When selecting icons, stick with silhouettes. Do not choose icons that are composed of outlines (iOS styled) or silhouettes knocked out of basic shapes.

Silhouette

Outlines

Outlines

Labeling

Choose an icon that is most directly associated to the label you are attaching it to.

Profile

ID

Security

Contours

Because our brand is heavily influenced by a rectangular grid, keep icon edges as sharp as possible. Slight rounding is acceptable when it more accurately portrays the real-world object it represents.

No rounding

Skeuomorphic rounding

Global rounding

Perspective

Keep icons flat and stay away from isometric, or angled, perspectivies.

Front-facing

Isometric

Level of detail

Icons should try to be as simplistic as possible. Look for icons that are just detailed enough to clearly convey their message.

Too abstract

Too realistic

Photography

The main imagery should represent the warmth and adventure of living, through active and engaging scences that are bright and personal.

These photos should properly reflect the demographics and diversity of Colonial Life's employees and policyholders while taking care to accurately reflect the content.

Supplemental photography

Not all content calls for photos of people. However "objective" imagery should match the style of the core lifestyle photography.
